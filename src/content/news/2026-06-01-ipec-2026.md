---
title: "Two Presentations at IPEC-ECCE Asia 2026 in Nagasaki"
date: 2026-06-01
---

The lab presented two papers at the 10th International Power Electronics Conference (IPEC-ECCE Asia 2026), held May 31 – June 4, 2026 in Nagasaki, Japan.

MSc student JT Luterbach presented "Fully Closed-Loop Control with Propagation Delay Compensation for the Soft-Switched Bidirectional Phase-Shifted Full Bridge Converter" (co-authors: Andrew M. Knight, Philippe Gray).

Dr. Gray presented "Three-Phase AC–DC Single-Stage MMC Enabled by Bidirectional-Switch Based Submodules with Ultra-Low-Capacitance" (co-authors: Khai Huynh, Flávio Alegretti Ramos, Phuc Luong).
