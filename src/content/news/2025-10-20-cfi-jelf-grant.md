---
title: "CFI JELF Grant for Medium-Voltage Test Facility"
date: 2025-10-20
---

Dr. Gray has received a Canada Foundation for Innovation John R. Evans Leaders Fund (CFI JELF) grant for the project "Advanced Medium-Voltage Test Facility for Next-Generation Power Electronics & Drives: Accelerating Clean Tech Adoption." The grant will support the development of infrastructure for testing advanced power electronics and drives, enabling research that accelerates the adoption of clean energy technologies. The award was part of a [UCalgary round of 19 CFI JELF-funded projects](https://ucalgary.ca/news/ucalgary-receives-more-47-million-funding-innovative-research) announced in October 2025.
