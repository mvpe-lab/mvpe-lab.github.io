We develop power electronics for electric vehicles and charging infrastructure,
including integrated onboard chargers that reuse the traction drive, bidirectional
three-phase AC chargers with buck-boost capability, and resonance-based wireless power
transfer systems. Our goal is charging hardware that is lighter, cheaper, and more
efficient, accelerating the adoption of electrified transport.
