Medium-voltage DC–DC conversion is a key enabling technology for DC grids, renewable
integration, and high-power charging. We research modular multilevel converter (MMC)
based DC–DC topologies — including high-frequency MMCs, current-shaping MMCs, and
current-fed isolated MMCs with soft switching — that achieve high step ratios and high
efficiency at medium voltage.
