Grid-scale energy storage is essential for a reliable, renewables-dominated power
system. We study power electronic interfaces for battery energy storage — from
converter topologies that connect storage directly at medium voltage to control
strategies that deliver grid services — with an emphasis on efficiency, power density,
and cost.
