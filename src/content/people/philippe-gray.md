Dr. Philippe Gray is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Software
Engineering at the Schulich School of Engineering, University of Calgary. He received his
B.A.Sc. in Engineering Science and his M.A.Sc. and Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from
the University of Toronto.

Prior to joining the University of Calgary, he was a visiting researcher at ABB Corporate
Research in Sweden, a systems design engineer at General Electric in the UK working on
HVDC transmission, and a senior power electronics engineer at Lucid Motors working on
onboard charger design. His research interests include transportation electrification,
solid-state transformers, and energy storage systems.
